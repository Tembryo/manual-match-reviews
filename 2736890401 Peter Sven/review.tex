\paragraph{Match Context}
Solo Ranked RD. Safelane carry Sven. Build an advantage through accelearated early farm, win midgame fights with the burst damage, build into a lategame force with strong AoE damage output. After a good laning stage entered fights slightly early and died, builds up a good position but game continues with alternating teamfight wins into very late game.

\paragraph{Highlight Tip}
Be aware of the big AoE ultimates on the enemy team - preventing spells like Reverse Polarity from hitting multiple heroes is crucial to win teamfights. Either fight only in areas with vision, or be careful to position away from allies.

\nnsection{Tips}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Check the inventory of enemies whenever possible. Spotting a Blink Dagger can be crucial for fights, e.g. \logref{15:27} \logref{20:04}.
    \item Prioritize farming until core items are finished, Sven has difficulty engaging without Blink Dagger. If you have to join fights, play safe.
\end{itemize}
