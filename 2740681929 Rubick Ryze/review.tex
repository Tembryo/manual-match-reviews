\paragraph{Match Context}
Professional game for Boston Major 2016 Qualifiers, Groupstage. Position 5 support Rubick. Focus is on warding and contributing to ganks and teamfights with spells. Had to play with small amount of farm as the game plays out evenly, major contribution with stolen Eclipse in two teamfights, game is lost in a misplayed teamfight around Rosh.

\begin{itemize}
    \item Missed early Tango Usage: \logref{1:51} \logref{7:19} (this one prevents death in a fight)
    \item Prioritise preventing sidepulls by the offlaner! Stood too far at \logref{2:21} \logref{3:21}

    \item Missed 26 lasthits to own creeps when pushing - prioritise lasthitting the creep that dies first to the own wave/ranged creep over getting slightly more push.
    \item Don't move close to the enemy cores with BKB in lategame fights, stay further out to cast spells. \logref{44:51} \logref{47:06} \logref{49:42}
\end{itemize}
